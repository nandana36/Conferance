About ICCSC 2026

LBS Institute of Technology for Women, established in 2001, is Kerala's first engineering college for women. Managed by LBS Centre for Science and Technology, it offers UG and PG programs in engineering and is a recognized research center under KTU.

ICCSC 2026, organized by the Departments of ECE & CSE, aims to promote innovations in intelligent computing and communication.

Where

LBS Intitute of Technology for Women, Trivandrum

When

January 28-30 2026

Get Your Ticket

Objectives

  • Knowledge Sharing Platform

    To provide a platform for researchers, academicians, and industry experts to share the latest advancements in computational systems and communication technologies.

  • Research Dissemination

    To facilitate presentation and publication of innovative research findings in areas such as wireless communication, 6G networks, artificial intelligence, computing systems, IoT, and network security.

  • Interdisciplinary Collaboration

    To encourage interdisciplinary collaborations for developing integrated solutions to modern technological challenges.
