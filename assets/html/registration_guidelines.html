Registration Guidelines for ICASET (International Conference on Advanced Science, Engineering, and Technology)

Registration Confirmation: Upon successful registration and payment, participants will receive a confirmation email with event details.

Payment Confirmation: Once the payment is confirmed, the participant will receive an acknowledgment email with further instructions.

Document Submission: All registered participants are required to submit necessary documents (e.g., ID proof, affiliation letter) before the specified deadline.
