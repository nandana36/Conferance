Submission guidelines


Whether you are submitting an abstract or a full research paper, make sure you follow our guidelines, tips, and terms. This can increase your chances of paper acceptance.


Abstract Submission Guidelines


    Abstract Structure:

  • ✅ Ensure your abstract is in English and doesn't exceed 500 words.

  • ✅ We encourage detailed abstracts that provide a comprehensive summary of your paper.

    Submission Process:

  • ✅ Conveniently submit your abstract through the provided link.

  • ✅ Expect a prompt decision on acceptance within 48 hours.

    Steps to Follow for Abstract Submission:

  • ✅ Submit your abstract online or via email to info@icaset.in.

  • ✅ Receive an acknowledgment email within three working days.

  • ✅ In case of no response, promptly contact us at info@icaset.in.

Full Paper Submission Guidelines


    1. Each paper should consist of three sections:

  • ✅ Background, Motivation, and Objective

  • ✅ Statement of Contribution/Methods

  • ✅ Results, Discussions, and Conclusions

  • 2. Maintain a total of 6 pages in a double-column format.

  • 3. Consider the total character count, excluding spaces, Titles, Author Names, and Affiliations..

    • After you've finished preparing your full paper according to the above instructions,submit your Full paper Here.
